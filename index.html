How it works

ChurnLogic focuses on intent and timing, not hype or shortcuts.

1. Add your cards

Users enter existing cards and open dates to establish eligibility constraints.

2. Set goals

Travel, cashback, or simplicity — recommendations adapt to user intent.

3. Follow a plan

ChurnLogic generates a clear application path with guardrails.

For affiliate and issuer partners

ChurnLogic drives high-intent, compliant traffic from users actively planning credit card applications.

  • No couponing or incentives
  • No manufactured spending advice
  • Clear affiliate disclosures
  • Recommendations based on eligibility and user goals
